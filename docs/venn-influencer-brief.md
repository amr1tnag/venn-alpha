# Venn — Find your people, then find your place

**One-liner:** Venn is a flatmate-matching app. Think of a dating app — but instead of finding a date, you find the right person to live with.

---

## The problem

Finding a flat in a big city is stressful. Finding a *flatmate* is worse.

Right now, people find flatmates through random Facebook groups, WhatsApp forwards, broker listings, or friends-of-friends. You end up living with a total stranger you know nothing about — their sleep schedule, cleanliness, food habits, whether they smoke, whether they're okay with your dog. The flat matters, but the *person* is what makes or breaks the experience.

## What Venn does

Venn matches people on **lifestyle compatibility** — the name comes from a Venn diagram: two circles, and the overlap between them.

- **Browse real profiles, not listings.** Every profile shows photos, age, budget, preferred areas, work/education, and personality prompts like *"My ideal noise level at home is…"* or *"I handle shared chores by…"* — so you get a feel for the person before you ever message them.
- **Set your preferences.** Area, budget, flat type, move-in date, gender, food habits, smoking, drinking, pets. Venn surfaces the people who overlap with what you need.
- **Like and match.** See someone compatible? Send a like — with a personal note if you want. If they like you back, it's a match and a chat opens up. No matches, no spam: only people who mutually chose each other can talk.
- **Standouts — for people who already have a flat.** Flat owners looking for a flatmate get their own showcase, with flat photos and rent shown upfront. Interested? Send them a **Key 🔑** to open the conversation.
- **Chat safely inside the app.** Real-time messaging with read receipts, typing indicators, and online status — no need to share your phone number until you're ready.
- **Safety built in.** Block and report from anywhere in the app. Blocking someone removes the conversation entirely, both ways.

## Who it's for

Students and young professionals moving to or within the city — currently focused on **Mumbai and Navi Mumbai** (Bandra, Andheri, Powai, and beyond). Two sides of the same coin:

1. **Flat-seekers** looking for a compatible person to team up with or move in with.
2. **Flat-owners** with a spare room, looking for a flatmate they'll actually get along with.

## Why now

Venn is in **beta and completely free** — unlimited likes, unlimited Keys. Early users get the full experience at no cost while we grow the community.

> "You'll spend more time with your flatmate than with most of your friends — Venn makes sure it's someone you actually overlap with."
